About Us


Code Club (CC) Academy is a leading Ed-tech/Health-tech institution dedicated to providing top-notch technology training, hosting educational programs, and supporting students' academic journeys. We specialize in developing assistive devices to improve the lives of persons with disabilities and offer a range of workshops and programs to empower and educate. We aim to foster innovation and inclusivity through cutting-edge technology and comprehensive learning experiences.

Mission
